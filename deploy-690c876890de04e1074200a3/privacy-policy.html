Privacy Policy for Lineach Detailing

Effective Date: January 2024

Lineach Detailing ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect the information you share with us, particularly through LinkedIn lead generation forms and our website https://lineach.com.

What We Collect

When you submit a form, we may collect:

  • Full Name
  • Email Address
  • Company Name (if applicable)
  • Phone Number (if provided)

We collect this information only when you voluntarily provide it through our lead generation forms, contact forms, or direct communication.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

We will never sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

Legal Basis (GDPR Compliance)

We process your data based on your consent when submitting a form. You have the right to withdraw consent or request deletion of your information at any time.

Data Storage

Your information is stored securely and only accessible to authorized personnel within Lineach Detailing. We retain your information only as long as necessary to fulfill your request or comply with legal requirements.

Your Rights

Under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold
  • Request correction or deletion
  • Withdraw consent at any time
  • Lodge a complaint with the Data Protection Commissioner (Ireland)

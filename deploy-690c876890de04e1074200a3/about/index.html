About Lineach

Delivering safe, compliant, and efficient piping and welding solutions across Ireland.

Who We Are

Lineach is an Irish engineering firm specialising in process, utility, and high-purity piping installations. Our teams include ASME IX/EN287 certified welders, pipe fitters, and project engineers who deliver turnkey projects with rigorous QA and safety controls.

Capabilities

  • Design, spooling, fabrication, installation, testing, and commissioning
  • Materials: Carbon Steel, Stainless, Duplex/Super Duplex, Inconel, Titanium, Hastelloy, CuNi
  • Welding: TIG, MIG, SMAW, SAW, Flux-Core, Open Root
  • Documentation: WPS/WPQR, material certs, weld maps, pressure tests
  • NDT: UT, RT, MT, PT on request

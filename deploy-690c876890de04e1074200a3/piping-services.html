End-to-End Mechanical Piping Contractors

At Lineach, our experienced mechanical piping contractors provide end-to-end solutions for transporting gases, chemicals, water, steam, and petroleum. With in-house pipe fitters, ASME IX & EN287 certified welders, inspectors, and engineers, we handle materials including Carbon Steel, Stainless Steel, Duplex/Super Duplex, Inconel, Titanium, Hastelloy, and Copper Nickel.

Our turnkey packages cover design, spooling, installation, testing, coating, insulation, and commissioning—delivered with rigorous safety and compliance.
